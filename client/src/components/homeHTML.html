What is NLP?

Natural Language Processing

The field of study that focuses on the interactions between human language and computers is called natural language processing , or NLP for short. It sits at the intersection of computer science, artificial intelligence, and computational linguistics ( Wikipedia ).

General NLP Overview

Focus of this Web Application

This web application focuses on allowing the user to experiment with NLP with no prior knowledge or experience needed. Through the tools created, the hope is to bring you closer to the code so that this can be a stepping stone into your own inquiry.

Bag of Words

By using your own text to explore what a bag of words is, you can learn the foundations of how a text becomes data. By learning these fundamentals, you are able to start exploring NLP on your own as well as better understand what tools already exist out there.
